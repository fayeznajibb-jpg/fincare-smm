## CHARACTER LIMITS — KNOW BEFORE YOU POST

| Element | Limit | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Caption | **2,200 characters** | Full caption including hashtags |
| Caption preview | **~125 characters** | Shown before "more" — this is your hook |
| Hashtags | **30 max** | Best practice: 10–15 |
| Slide text | **No strict limit** | Keep under 100 chars per slide for readability |
| Bio | **150 characters** | Where your link in bio lives |
