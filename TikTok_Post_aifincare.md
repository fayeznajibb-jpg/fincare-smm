## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Style:** Confident, direct, slightly humorous. Think: sharp friend giving real talk — not a hype reel.
**Creator tip:** Start speaking mid-sentence. No "hey guys", no slow intro. TikTok punishes slow hooks.
**Pacing:** New text overlay every 3–4 seconds. Cut on beat if using music.
**B-roll ideas:**
- Phone screen with portfolio in red — person's face reacting
- Dark room, 2am vibe, phone glowing
- Fincare app UI — anxiety score dropping from red to calm
- Chart recovering after a dip

**Trending formats to use:**
- `"POV: You finally stopped letting fear make your investing decisions"`
- `"Things the 1% of traders know that the 99% hide"`
- `"Nobody posts this side of investing"`

**Repurpose:** This script works as an Instagram Reel with no changes.
**Comment strategy:** Reply to the top 3 comments with a follow-up video response — doubles your reach.
