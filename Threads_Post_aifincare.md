# Threads Post — aifincare.com

**Platform:** Threads (@threads)
**Goal:** Brand awareness + drive profile visits → link in bio → aifincare.com
**Format:** Short text post (single or thread chain)
**Best Time to Post:** Monday–Friday, 9am–12pm or 6–9pm
**Voice:** Sharp. Punchy. Conversational. Like a smart thought you had mid-coffee.

---

## CHARACTER LIMITS — KNOW BEFORE YOU POST

| Element | Limit | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Single post | **500 characters** | Text only or text + media |
| Thread chain | **500 chars per post** | Chain up to 10 posts |
| Images per post | **10 max** | JPG, PNG, WebP |
| Video | **Up to 5 minutes** | MP4 recommended |
| Bio | **150 characters** | Same as Instagram bio |
| Hashtags | **Not algorithm-driven** | Use 1–2 max, sparingly |

> ⚠️ Threads does NOT boost posts via hashtags the way Instagram does.
> Reach is driven by **engagement quality** — replies, quotes, reshares.
> Write to start a conversation, not to broadcast.

## POSTING NOTES

- **No link in post body** on Threads — put it in bio and reference "link in bio"
- **End with a question** whenever possible — Threads rewards replies heavily
- **Quote + reshare** posts that engage with your content — boosts reach significantly
- **Thread chains** outperform single posts for educational content
- **Tone:** More casual than LinkedIn, more thoughtful than TikTok
- **Reply to every comment** in the first hour — Threads algorithm is reply-driven
